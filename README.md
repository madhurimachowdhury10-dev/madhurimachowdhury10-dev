### ✦ Hi, I'm Madhurima 👋

Ten years. Three disciplines. Content that actually works.

I specialise in **UX Writing**, **Technical Writing**, and **Content Strategy** for complex, globally scaled products. My work sits at the intersection of user pain points, information architecture, and the words on screen that determine whether a user self-resolves or files a support ticket.

I have led corpus-level revamps, redesigned error copy, built prioritisation frameworks from escalation data, and shipped content across Product, UX, Engineering, and Support teams that moves real metrics.

- ✍️ **UX Writing:** Error messages, tooltips, in-product guidance, microcopy
- 📖 **Technical Writing:** Help Center docs, structured authoring, content modelling
- 📐 **Content Strategy:** content audits, pain point analysis, information architecture, AI-augmented workflows
